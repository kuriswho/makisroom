Scones:

The only ones I like. An old teacher gave me her recipe.
Ingredients:

  • 225g Plain flour
  • 150ml Milk
  • 1tsp Baking Powder
  • 25g Butter
  • Pinch of Salt

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C
  2. Sieve flour, salt & baking powder into a large bowl
  3. Rub in butter until a breadcrumb-like consistency is reached
  4. Add milk, kneading until a soft, shaggy dough forms
  5. Separate into 2.5cm cubes
  6. Place on a floured baking sheet
  7. Optionally glaze with beaten egg
  8. Bake for 10 mins
  9. Allow to cool on a wire tray and serve with butter/cream and fruit jam
