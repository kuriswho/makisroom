Couscous Stuffed Courgette:

They only sell courgettes in twos now, so I always have one spare.
Here's how I use up the whole thing in a handy weeknight dinner.
Ingredients:

  • 1 Courgette
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Couscous
  • Spices of choice
  • 1 Tbsp Cream cheese
  • 1 Tbsp Honey
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Balsamic Dressing

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 220C
  2. Slice the courgette in half lengthways
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt & pepper
  4. Place cut side down on some foil in the oven for 15 mins
  5. Meanwhile, make coucous, adding spices and optional stock to the water (I use cumin, paprika and chilli)
  6. Mix in the cream cheese, honey and parmesan
  7. When you take the courgette out, turn it cut-side-up and score it well with a fork
  8. Leave the oven on but turn it down to 180C
  9. Pile the couscous mix into the courgette halves, and return to the oven for another 5-10 mins
  10. Finish with a drizzle of balsamic dressing
