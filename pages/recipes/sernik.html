Sernik:

Polish cheesecake, according to my old landlord's mum's recipe.
Ingredients:

  • 200g Cream Cheese
  • 4 Eggs
  • 200ml Yogurt
  • 3 Tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 200g Frozen Cherries/Raspbebbies

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Separate egg whites and whip to stiff peaks.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix cream cheese, yogurt, sugar, flour and vanilla.
  4. Fold egg whites into the cheese.
  5. Line a 9" baking tin with baking paper (sides and bottom.)
  6. Transfer cheese into tin.
  7. Spread the frozen fruit on top.
  8. Bake for ~1hr at 180C.
  9. It will expand in the oven but it will collapse later don't panic.
