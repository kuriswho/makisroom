Paneer Skewers:

An old housemate made these for a last- minute barbecue once and I've loved them ever since.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 200g Diced Paneer Cheese
  • 1 Cup Dark soy sauce
  • 1 Cup Yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Honey
  • 2 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Chilli Powder/Paste
  • 1 Tsp Garam Masala

Method:

  1. Chop the pepper and the onion roughly
  2. Mix all of the ingredients except for the paneer cheese and chopped vegetables in a large tupperware
  3. Once homogenous, add the paneer and veg, mixing to coat thoroughly
  4. Allow to marinade in the fridge for at least an hour (up to overnight)
  5. Skewer with bamboo and cook on a barbecue until the veg are crispy and the paneer is cooked through
